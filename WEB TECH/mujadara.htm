Mujadara

Description

Mujadara is a comforting Arabic dish made of lentils and rice topped with caramelized onions. It's hearty, healthy, and full of flavor.

This vegetarian recipe is perfect for a satisfying meal and pairs beautifully with yogurt or spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown basmati rice
  • 1 cup green lentils
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1.75 tsp sea salt
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 5 cups water

Steps

  1. Boil water with garlic, bay leaves, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add rice and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add lentils and cook until tender.
  4. Caramelize onions in olive oil separately.
  5. Combine rice and lentils, top with onions, and serve.
