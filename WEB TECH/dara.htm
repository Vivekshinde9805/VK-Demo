Creamy Dara

Description

Creamy Dara is a delightful Filipino crepe dessert filled with sweet coconut and cream. It's a popular treat in the Visayas region, known for its soft texture and rich flavor.

This version uses Nestlé All Purpose Cream to enhance the richness of both the crepe and the coconut filling.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1 packet Nestlé All Purpose Cream
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups grated coconut
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Whisk water, cream, flour, egg, oil, and vanilla. Strain and rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Cook crepes in a non-stick pan, flipping once.
  3. Prepare filling by cooking coconut, cream, water, and sugar until dry.
  4. Fill each crepe with 2 tbsp of filling, fold and roll tightly.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
