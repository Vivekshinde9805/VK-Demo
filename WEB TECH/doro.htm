Doro Wat

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 3 cups onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 cup berbere paste
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in lemon juice and salt.
  2. Pur√©e onions, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Cook butter, paprika, and berbere paste.
  4. Add onion mix and reduce.
  5. Add stock, wine, and chicken. Simmer until tender.
  6. Add eggs and cook 10 more minutes. Serve hot.
